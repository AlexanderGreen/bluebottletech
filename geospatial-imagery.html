PRODUCTS : geospatial imagery

Global and site-specific geospatial datasets with extensive analysis and monitoring services.

The assessment, quantification, management and exploitation of natural resources can be challenging, especially when they are located in remote regions of the world. Despite this, geospatial technologies can provide the means to manage tracts of land and resources efficiently and effectively, whatever the management regime dictates are. For example, multispectral imagery from aircraft and satellite can be used to locate and quantify specific resources (even to the level of individual, high-value trees), map the terrain and transportation infrastructure, and measure exploitation of the resource over time.

When coupled with a comprehensive GIS, exploitation can be quantified, scheduled and monitored; resources can be mapped and quantified; and land management plans can be executed according to schedule and plan.

Blue Bottle Technologies provides both global and site-specific geospatial datasets for projects around the world, often tailored specifically to meet customer requirements. We map land use change, identify critical wildlife habitat, map wetlands, measure forest resources, map geologic structures, monitor crops, predict the weather, advise farmers and advise mineral exploration and mining operations to name just a few.
