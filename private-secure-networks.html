SOLUTIONS : private secure networks

The mobile phone has become the telephony device of choice.

The primary application for a Private Mobile Network is to provide Fixed Mobile Convergence ensuring mobile phones operate as an extension to a PBX. Our solutions are not limited to an office environment, being deployed as fixed infrastructure for static networks, and hand or vehicle mobile platforms for rapid deployment in areas without cellular coverage.

Our hand portable "SpeedyCell" units come in a single peli case that will fit into a standard aircraft overhead luggage compartment.

These units are designed for rapid deployment, with a network being available in as little as 5 minutes. Our vehicular units provide the same rapid deployment as our hand portable units, but the availability of increased power allows increased run-time and greater range.

Blue Bottle Technologies offers a variety of back-haul and interconnect options via satellite, WiFi and direct interconnect to the mobile operator for fully inter-operable extension of the operator's mobile network.
