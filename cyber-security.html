PRODUCTS : cyber security

In the USA the "cyber threat is one of the most serious economic and national security challenges we face as a nation" and that "America's economic prosperity in the 21st Century will depend on cyber security."
President Barack Obama

Blue Bottle Technologies offer consultancy and training services designed to protect IT infrastructure from security risks.

The openness and connectivity of the digital economy today provides new opportunities but also creates huge risks. Lack of security provision results in these risks being exploited, with average discovery of a cyber attack 404 days later, and the loss of data coupled with financial and reputational loss, this is the tier 1 threat facing business and national security today.

We take a structured approach to assessing business and IT risks and provide consultancy and support

as appropriate, answering key questions such as:

  • How many attempts have already been made to compromise your systems?
  • To what extent are the assets or processes exposed?
  • What are the consequences and costs of a successful attack?

We provide a total service covering, vulnerability assessment, penetration testing, physical security assessment, WiFi security assessment, incident response, forensic & information security readiness planning, and data recovery.

Our consultants are QSTM Tiger qualified and all penetration tests and vulnerability assessments are conducted to CESG certified standards, with web application testing undertaken in-line with the OWASP methodology.
